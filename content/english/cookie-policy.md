##### What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device when you visit them. They allow the site to remember your preferences and analyse how content is used.

##### Cookies used on this site

| Type | Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Technical | `cookieconsent_status` | Saves your cookie consent preference | 2 days |
| Analytics | `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Google Analytics — measures traffic anonymously | 2 years / 1 year |

Analytics cookies are only activated if you accept their use. No advertising or marketing cookies are used.

##### Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Information collected is processed anonymously. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data it holds.

- Google Privacy Policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Google Analytics opt-out add-on: [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

##### How to manage or disable cookies

You can configure your browser to block or delete cookies at any time. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect how the site works.

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Preferences → Privacy & Security
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions
